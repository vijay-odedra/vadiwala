Our Story

Vadiwala began as a small family kitchen in 2005, with Grandma Asha's secret recipes that had been passed down through generations. What started as homemade meals for friends and neighbors quickly grew into a beloved local food destination.

Today, we continue to honor those traditions while embracing innovation, bringing you the authentic flavors of home-cooked meals with the convenience of modern life.

Our Mission

At Vadiwala, our mission is simple: to deliver delicious, authentic, and nutritious food that brings people together. We believe that good food is not just about taste, but about the memories created around the table.

We're committed to using locally-sourced, fresh ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create meals that nourish both body and soul.
